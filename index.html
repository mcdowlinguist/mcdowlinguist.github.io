welcome

Here you’ll find information about me, my research and various projects, as well as the occasional blog post.

I’m a linguist working in Montréal with a specialization in experimental phonology, and I am also currently one of the co-editors of the Revue canadienne de linguistique/Canadian Journal of Linguistics.
